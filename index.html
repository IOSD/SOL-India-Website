A Little About Us!

Sol India is committed to delivering clean, profitable and sustainable solar solutions to businesses and households. We believe in building long lasting relationships with our customers. Merging your interests with clean technology giving you a sustainable competitive edge.

Why Choose Solar Energy ?


  • Environment-friendly as there is no emission of greenhouse gases
  • Source of ongoing free energy- inexhaustible and the best replacement for other non- renewable sources
  • Averse to fuel price fluctuation
  • Enjoy considerably lower energy bills
  • Export surplus energy produces to the utility grid and get it credited to your energy bills for later use
  • Suitable for all household needs in rural as well as urban areas
