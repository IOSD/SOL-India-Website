Feed-In
Tariffs

See, even the government supports you to switch to solar! These government subsidized, cost cutting discounts (feed-in tariffs) are just a few of advantages, that using a green energy gives you… Basically it comes in a shape of the payment made to households or businesses generating their own electricity through the use of methods that do not contribute to the depletion of natural resources, proportional to the amount of power generated.

Savings Rate calculated by subtracting PPA or equivalent lease kWh rate from relevant utility kWh rate.
